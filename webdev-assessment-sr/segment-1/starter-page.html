Frequently Asked Questions

What is an AI landing page builder?

An AI landing page builder is software that creates landing pages using artificial intelligence instead of manual design or templates. Website Studio applies this by generating page structure, layout, and content from a prompt.

How does an AI landing page builder work?

Website Studio's AI analyzes your prompt to understand your business, audience, and goals, then generates a complete page structure with hero sections, feature blocks, testimonials, CTAs, and more — all optimized for conversion and SEO.

Who is Website Studio built for?

Website Studio is built for marketers, agencies, and growth teams who need to create and launch high-performing landing pages at scale — without needing developers or design tools.

Do I need coding experience to use Website Studio?

No coding is required. Website Studio is designed for marketers and non-technical users. You describe what you need in plain language, and the AI handles the rest — from page structure to publishing.
