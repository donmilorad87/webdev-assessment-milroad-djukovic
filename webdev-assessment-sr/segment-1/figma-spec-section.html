Getting Started with Website Studio AI Landing Page Builder
gap: 12px

Start in Website Studio by describing the page you want to create. Once submitted, the landing page creator generates the full layout and sections, ready for editing and publishing in minutes.

margin-top: 48px
01
Describe your page in a single prompt

Describe your page in plain language — include your product, audience, location, style, colors, and key elements. Website Studio generates a complete page structure aligned with your intent.

02
Edit with commands or visual controls

Update copy, visuals, layout, or sections through commands or visual controls. Skip this step when the page already matches your campaign.

03
Publish and Launch instantly

Connect your domain and go live on CDN-hosted pages with free SSL, tracking scripts, and lead capture already in place — no hosting setup or external tools required.
